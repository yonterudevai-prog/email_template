1 · How to Search on Portals

Step 1 — Search keyword Paste copied role title (e.g. ".NET Developer") in search box. తెలుగు: middle nundi role copy chesi portal search lo paste cheyandi
Step 2 — Location ⚠️ (easy to get wrong) Geographic location: Type United States for nationwide — or exact city/state (e.g. Dallas, TX · New Jersey · California). Work mode filter: Select ALL or check every option — Remote · Hybrid · On-site · In-person. Do NOT filter Remote only — you will miss hybrid & onsite jobs! ⚠️ Wrong country/spelling = no jobs. Use United States not "USA"/"US". Work mode: keep Remote + Hybrid + Onsite all selected for maximum results. తెలుగు: location lo "United States" or city name exact ga type cheyandi. Work mode lo Remote, Hybrid, Onsite anni select cheyandi — Remote matrame filter cheyakandi, chala jobs miss avutayi
Step 3 — Date posted filter ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT Always select: Last 24 hours or Past 3 days — apply to fresh jobs first! ⚠️ Skip jobs older than 3 days — already filled or too much competition. తెలుగు: "Last 24 hours" / "Past 3 days" filter — kotha jobs ki fast ga apply cheyandi. Purana jobs skip cheyandi.
Step 4 — Job type filter Select: Full-time · Contract · W2 (if option available) తెలుగు: Full-time select cheyandi · contract kuda ok
Step 5 — Experience level ⚠️ Entry level / Mid level — match your experience ⚠️ Do NOT pick Senior if you have 0–2 yrs — application gets rejected fast. తెలుగు: mee experience ki match avvela filter pettandi — Senior select cheyakandi (0-2 yrs unte)
Step 6 — Easy Apply / Quick Apply Turn ON "Easy Apply" filter (Indeed/LinkedIn) — faster applications తెలుగు: Easy Apply filter ON — easy ga resume submit avutundi

3 · Tips for Better Interview Calls

✅ Apply within 1–2 hours of job posting Fresh jobs = less competition. Check portals 2–3 times daily (morning, afternoon, night). తెలుగు: job post ayyaka 1-2 hours lopu apply cheyandi — interview chance ekkuva
✅ Apply 10–20 jobs per day More applications = more recruiter calls. Target: 10–20 per day. తెలుగు: roju 10-20 jobs ki apply cheyandi
✅ Match resume keywords to job description Add skills from job post to your resume before applying. తెలుగు: job description lo unna skills resume lo add cheyandi
✅ Keep LinkedIn profile updated Photo, headline, skills, "Open to Work" ON — recruiters search LinkedIn directly. తెలుగు: LinkedIn profile complete ga pettandi · "Open to Work" enable cheyandi
✅ Answer recruiter call immediately ⚠️ Unknown US number? Pick up — could be recruiter. Call back within 30 min if missed. ⚠️ Missing recruiter call = lost interview chance. Keep phone on during US hours. తెలుగు: recruiter call vaste immediate attend cheyandi · miss aithe 30 min lopu call back
✅ Avoid jobs posted 30+ days ago Old postings may already be filled. Focus on Last 24 hours / Past 3 days only. తెలుగు: 30 days purana jobs skip cheyandi — already fill ayy untayi
✅ Save search + turn on job alerts Indeed/LinkedIn/Dice — save search → email alert ON when new job matches. తెలుగు: job alert ON cheyandi — kotha job vachinapudu email vastundi
